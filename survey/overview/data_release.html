Data Release

The first DELVE data release (DELVE DR1) is hosted at the Astro Data Lab and consists of catalog-level coadds in g,r,i,z from the DELVE-WIDE processing. It covers ~5,000 deg2 of the southern sky and contains ~520 million unique objects.

Browse the Data Access page (side menu) to find out about different ways to use the DELVE DR1 catalog or obtain DELVE image cutouts.
